Tareque Robinson

Tareque Robinson is a visionary who seeks to bring to life his many conceptualizations and ideas. Ideas are vast and endless but not until one acts upon an idea will it be anything of significance. This is a very important thing to bear in mind as it allows one to undertand the importance of taking action towards fulfilling goals instead of being stuck in a so-called 'planning phase'. One has to develop and deploy a way of thinking which gives room for error, a way of thinking that appreciates that failure is merely a path towards eventual and inevitable success.
